The B2DROP service is still free to use, but limited to scientific use-cases. To request access to B2DROP, follow this steps:

How to request access:

  • Login at b2access.eudat.eu with the same account you would like to be activated for B2DROP
  • Go to the "profile" section
  • Copy and paste the "voPersonID"
  • Contact us sending a short description of your use case and your "voPersonID"

Your request is going to be validated and we come back to you.
